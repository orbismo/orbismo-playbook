# Romance (genre pack)

The relationship arc as trackable machinery, for a [Novel Studio](../../) novel. It adds three thread types to the studio's promise/payoff ledger — `spark` (moments of connection that the ending should echo), `wound` (the belief keeping each lead from love, which must be confronted before the happy ending), and `rift` (the fault line that erupts at the dark moment and that the grand gesture must actually answer) — and four review lenses: Dual-POV Balance, the Relationship Ladder (intimacy never jumps a rung offstage), Wound Work, and the Earned Ending.

Requires the Novel Studio app. Install by giving [pack.json](pack.json) to your connected AI; then say "adopt the Romance pack for \<novel\>" (recorded in that novel's Blueprint). From there, "has anything pressed on her wound yet?" is a query, and reviews check that the ending is earned rather than asserted.

Packs are data, not rules — this installs one `reference` entity (`reference/genre_pack_romance`) and touches nothing else.
