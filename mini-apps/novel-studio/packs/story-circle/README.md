# Story Circle (structure pack)

Dan Harmon's 8-step circle — the monomyth compressed to a wheel — as an adoptable [Novel Studio](../../) template: You, Need, Go, Search, Find, Take, Return, Change. Descend into the unfamiliar, pay for what you find, come back changed. Its even eighths and character-first framing make it a natural fit for episodic and character-driven stories, and it's light enough to run on a single chapter.

Requires the Novel Studio app. Install by giving [pack.json](pack.json) to your connected AI; then say "use the Story Circle for \<novel\>" to adopt it (the studio records the adoption in that novel's Blueprint). Scenes tagged with these step names light up the structure lens in reviews.

Packs are data, not rules — this installs one `reference` entity (`reference/beat_sheet_story_circle`) and touches nothing else.
