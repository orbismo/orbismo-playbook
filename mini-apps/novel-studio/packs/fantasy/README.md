# Fantasy (genre pack)

The genre's promises as trackable machinery, for a [Novel Studio](../../) novel. It adds four thread types to the studio's promise/payoff ledger — `prophecy` (its exact wording recorded, its payoff judged against it), `oath` (kept or broken on the page, with the cost shown), `artifact` (the sword on the wall must matter), and `heritage` (the hidden lineage, revealed or deliberately buried) — and four review lenses: the **Magic Cost Check** (power always pays its stated price), the **Prophecy Ledger**, **Power Creep** (no late-arriving abilities solving act 3), and **Travel Time** (the map doesn't shrink when the plot hurries).

The lenses lean on the core's **canon rules**: the laws of your world (what magic costs, what wards hold) recorded as `rule` entities with `rule_type: "canon"` and listed in the novel's index. Say "the rule of magic is…" and the studio records it; the Magic Cost Check then enforces it. A novel with no canon rules still works — those lenses report "no canon rules recorded" once and move on.

Requires the Novel Studio app. Install by giving [pack.json](pack.json) to your connected AI; then say "adopt the Fantasy pack for \<novel\>" (recorded in that novel's Blueprint). Pairs naturally with the [Hero's Journey](../heros-journey/) beat sheet.

Packs are data, not rules — this installs one `reference` entity (`reference/genre_pack_fantasy`) and touches nothing else.
