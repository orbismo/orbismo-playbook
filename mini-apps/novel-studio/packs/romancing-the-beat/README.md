# Romancing the Beat (structure pack)

The romance genre's beat sheet — the four-phase arc in the tradition of Gwen Hayes's *Romancing the Beat*, condensed here to sixteen beats — as an adoptable [Novel Studio](../../) template: Setup (Meet Cute, No Way, Adhesion), Falling in Love, Retreating from Love, and Fighting for Love (Epiphany, Grand Gesture, Happily Ever After). It structures the romance itself; the external plot runs alongside and may follow another sheet.

Requires the Novel Studio app. Install by giving [pack.json](pack.json) to your connected AI; then say "use Romancing the Beat for \<novel\>" to adopt it (the studio records the adoption in that novel's Blueprint). Pairs naturally with the [Romance](../romance/) genre pack — its `wound` threads track the very wounds and lies these beats turn on.

Packs are data, not rules — this installs one `reference` entity (`reference/beat_sheet_romancing_the_beat`) and touches nothing else.
