# Seven-Point Structure (structure pack)

Dan Wells's seven-point system as an adoptable [Novel Studio](../../) template: Hook, two plot turns, two pinch points, Midpoint, Resolution. Its signature move is plotting backwards — settle the Resolution first, write the Hook as its opposite, and let the five points between carry the change — which makes it a favorite of outliners who find fifteen-beat sheets too prescriptive.

Requires the Novel Studio app. Install by giving [pack.json](pack.json) to your connected AI; then say "use the Seven-Point Structure for \<novel\>" to adopt it (the studio records the adoption in that novel's Blueprint). When outlining with this sheet, the studio asks for your ending first — that's the method.

Packs are data, not rules — this installs one `reference` entity (`reference/beat_sheet_seven_point_structure`) and touches nothing else.
